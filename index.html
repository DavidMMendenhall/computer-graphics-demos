Computer Graphics Demos

A collection of interactive WebGL/WebGPU demonstrations

Frustum Culling

Frustum Culling

Demonstrates the technique of frustum culling to improve rendering performance by not rendering objects outside the camera's view. Includes interactive camera controls and visualization of the culling process.

View Demo
Ray Tracing Data Structures

Ray Tracing Data Structures

Explores the Bounding Volume Hierarchy (BVH) and Octree data structures used in ray tracing for efficient rendering. Click anywhere to cast a ray and visualize the intersection tests.

View Demo
Deferred Rendering

Deferred Rendering

Showcases the deferred rendering technique with WebGPU, which separates geometry and lighting calculations for complex scenes with many dynamic lights.
